What is the process for getting artificial turf installed at my Mesa property?
Our process starts with a free, on-site consultation where we measure your space, discuss turf options, and provide a detailed quote. Once you approve, we schedule your installation. On installation day, we remove existing grass, grade and compact the soil, install a weed barrier, add a layer of crushed rock for drainage, and then lay and secure your new turf. We finish by infilling and brushing the turf for a natural look. You’ll get a walkthrough at the end to ensure you’re satisfied with the results.
